ABOUT THE BANK

The Republica Bank was a retail and commercial bank tracing its roots back to 1863. Over the years, the bank operated under several names including The First National Bank of Chicago and First Chicago NBD (following its 1995 merger with the former National Bank of Detroit). In 1998, The Republica NBD merged with Banc One Corporation to form Bank One Corporation, today a part of Chase.
